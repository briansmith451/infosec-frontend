About & Help

This is an AI-powered decision-support tool providing immediate, accurate, and traceable guidance. It performs initial, time-consuming research to provide a consistent, auditable recommendation based directly on foundational doctrine and specific SCGs.

This tool is built on a "Human-in-the-Loop" philosophy and is intended to assist Security Managers and Classification Authorities, not replace them.

For questions, technical issues, or to report an incorrect analysis, please contact the help desk.
